Brewkaa Cafe is set to become Indore's premier destination for coffee lovers and those seeking a cozy, sophisticated ambiance. About Brewkaa Cafe

Brewkaa Cafe was born from a vision to create a unique, vegetarian-only coffee house that blends modern sophistication with the warmth of Indore's cultural heritage. Founded by Mr. Daksh Hande (CEO) and Mr. Ayush Tiwari (Co-Founder), Brewkaa Cafe is a testament to their shared love for coffee, community, and culinary excellence.

Our journey began with a simple idea: to craft a space where every guest feels at home, sipping ethically sourced coffee while enjoying delicious vegetarian dishes. Set to open in Bhavarkuva, Indore, in early 2026, Brewkaa Cafe is designed to be a haven for students, professionals, and families alike.

Our commitment to quality is unwavering. From single-origin coffee beans to locally sourced ingredients, every detail is curated to ensure an exceptional experience. The name "Brewkaa" is a registered trademark, symbolizing our dedication to authenticity and innovation.
